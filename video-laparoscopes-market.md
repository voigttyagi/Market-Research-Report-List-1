<p><p>The video laparoscopes market is divided into rigid and flexible types, catering to different surgical needs. Rigid laparoscopes offer high-quality imaging and are commonly used in abdominal surgeries, providing stability and precision. In contrast, flexible laparoscopes are designed for accessing difficult-to-reach areas within the body, making them ideal for procedures such as gynecological and urological surgeries. The choice between the two types depends on the specific requirements of the procedure, the anatomical location, and surgeon preference.</p></p>
