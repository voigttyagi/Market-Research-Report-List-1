<p><p>The Automatic Boom Barrier System market comprises two main types: hydraulic barriers and electromechanical barriers. Hydraulic barriers operate using fluid power, providing smooth and efficient movement, ideal for heavy-duty applications requiring robust performance. Electromechanical barriers utilize electric motors and mechanical components for operation; they offer faster opening and closing cycles and are suitable for light to medium traffic conditions. Both systems enhance security and traffic management in various settings, including parking lots, toll booths, and restricted access areas.</p></p>
