<p><p>Multilayer Ceramic Capacitors (MLCCs) are widely used passive components, essential for electronic circuits. They come in various types based on temperature stability and capacitance values. X7R offers good capacitance stability over a broad temperature range, while X5R provides moderate stability but can handle higher capacitances. C0G (NP0) is stable with minimal capacitance variation, ideal for precision applications. Y5V has a higher capacitance variation with temperature, making it less stable. Other types cater to specific needs, expanding MLCC versatility in industries.</p></p>
