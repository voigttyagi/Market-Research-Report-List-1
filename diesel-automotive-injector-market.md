<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Diesel Automotive Injector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Throttle Body Injection</li><li>Multipoint Injection</li><li>Direct Injection</li><li>Common Rail Injection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The diesel automotive injector market comprises several types, including Throttle Body Injection (TBI), which sprays fuel into the throttle body for air mixing before entering the engine; Multipoint Injection (MPI), where injectors deliver fuel directly into each cylinder for improved efficiency; Direct Injection (DI), which injects fuel directly into the combustion chamber for enhanced performance; and Common Rail Injection (CRI), where a high-pressure rail supplies fuel to multiple injectors, allowing for precise timing and better combustion control, improving overall engine efficiency and emissions.</p></p>
