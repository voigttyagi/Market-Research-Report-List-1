<p><p>The GSM Interception System market is divided into two primary types: Active and Passive Interception Systems. Active Interception Systems involve direct interaction with the mobile network, enabling the interception of live calls and messages through manipulation of the network infrastructure. In contrast, Passive Interception Systems monitor and capture data without altering the network, often analyzing metadata and communication patterns. Both systems serve security agencies and intelligence organizations, with applications in surveillance, law enforcement, and national security operations.</p></p>
