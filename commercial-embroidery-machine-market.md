<p><p>The commercial embroidery machine market is primarily divided into two types: single-head and multi-head embroidery machines. Single-head machines are designed for smaller scale operations, allowing for detailed customization in limited quantities, ideal for small businesses or startups. In contrast, multi-head machines can operate multiple embroidery heads simultaneously, greatly increasing production efficiency and scalability for larger operations. They are suitable for high-volume orders, making them popular in industries requiring mass customization and quick turnaround times.</p></p>
