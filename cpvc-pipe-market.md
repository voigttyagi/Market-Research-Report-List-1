<p><p>The CPVC pipe market primarily features two main types: Schedule 40 and Schedule 80 CPVC pipes. Schedule 40 CPVC pipes are designed for residential and light commercial use, offering a balance of strength and ductility, ideal for water handling applications. Schedule 80 CPVC pipes, on the other hand, are thicker and provide greater pressure ratings, making them suitable for industrial applications and services involving higher temperature and pressure conditions. Both types are popular for their corrosion resistance and durability.</p></p>
