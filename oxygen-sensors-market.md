<p><ul><li>Constant Potential Electrolysis Sensor</li><li>Galvanic Cell Type Gas Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oxygen sensors market is primarily characterized by two types: Constant Potential Electrolysis Sensors and Galvanic Cell Type Gas Sensors. Constant Potential Electrolysis Sensors utilize a controlled potential to monitor oxygen levels, providing high sensitivity and stability in various applications. Galvanic Cell Type Sensors operate through a chemical reaction that generates an electric current proportional to the oxygen concentration, making them widely used in environmental and industrial monitoring. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring safety and efficiency in diverse sectors.</p></p>
