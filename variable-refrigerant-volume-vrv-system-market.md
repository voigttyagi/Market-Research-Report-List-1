Heat Recovery Systems simultaneously provide heating and cooling to different zones by transferring excess heat from one space to another. In contrast, Heat Pump Systems focus on either heating or cooling based on the seasonal needs, offering flexibility and efficiency in temperature management. Both systems cater to varying demands in commercial and residential applications, enhancing energy efficiency and comfort.</p></p>
