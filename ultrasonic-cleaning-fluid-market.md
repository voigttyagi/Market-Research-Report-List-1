<p><p>The Ultrasonic Cleaning Fluid market is segmented into Water-Based and Solvent-Based types. Water-Based fluids are eco-friendly, non-flammable, and ideal for general cleaning, using water as the primary solvent with added detergents or surfactants. They are cost-effective and suitable for industries like healthcare and electronics. Solvent-Based fluids, containing chemicals like hydrocarbons or alcohols, are more aggressive, effective for removing tough contaminants like grease or oil. They are preferred in automotive and industrial applications but require careful handling due to flammability and environmental concerns.</p></p>
