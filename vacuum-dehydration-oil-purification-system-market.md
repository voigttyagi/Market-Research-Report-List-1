<p><p>The vacuum dehydration oil purification system (VDOPS) market comprises two main types: high-pressure and low-pressure systems. High-pressure VDOPS operates at elevated pressures to enhance oil purification efficiency, effectively removing water and contaminants, making it suitable for heavy-duty applications. Conversely, low-pressure VDOPS functions at atmospheric pressure, typically used for lighter oils and less critical applications, offering a cost-effective solution. Both systems are essential for maintaining oil quality and extending the lifespan of machinery in various industrial sectors.</p></p>
