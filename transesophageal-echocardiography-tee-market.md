<p><p>Transesophageal Echocardiography (TEE) is an advanced imaging technique that provides detailed and high-resolution views of the heart and its structures by inserting a probe into the esophagus. This method allows for better visualization of cardiac conditions compared to standard transthoracic echocardiography, especially in patients with poor acoustic windows. TEE is essential in assessing complex cardiac issues, guiding surgical procedures, and monitoring patients during and after cardiac surgery.</p><p>The TEE market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the increasing prevalence of cardiovascular diseases, technological advancements, and a growing aging population. Hospitals and healthcare facilities are adopting TEE due to its ability to deliver accurate diagnostic information, enhancing patient outcomes. Rising awareness about the importance of early detection of heart conditions and improvements in healthcare infrastructure are also contributing to market expansion.
