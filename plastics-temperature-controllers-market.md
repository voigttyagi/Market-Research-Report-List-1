<p><p>The plastics temperature controllers market is divided into two main types: mechanical and electronic. Mechanical temperature controllers use mechanical components to regulate temperature, offering a simple and reliable solution for basic applications. They typically involve bimetallic strips or liquid-filled sensing elements. In contrast, electronic temperature controllers utilize sensors and digital displays to provide precise temperature control, enabling more advanced functionalities, such as programmable settings and real-time monitoring. Both types serve various industries, catering to different operational needs and complexities.</p></p>
