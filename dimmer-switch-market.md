<p><p>The dimmer switch market features various types tailored to specific applications. Rheostat dimmers utilize resistive elements to control light levels but are less efficient. Coil-rotation transformers adjust voltage through magnetic coils, offering better energy efficiency. Solid-state dimmers use electronic components for precise control and are widely favored for their reliability and energy savings. Other types may include smart dimmers with connectivity features, allowing for remote control and automation, further expanding the market's versatility and appeal.</p></p>
