<p><p>Accounting software systems are categorized into several market types. Installed software is locally operated on individual computers, ensuring control but often requiring manual updates. SaaS (Software as a Service) delivers applications via subscription, accessible online without installation. Cloud-based solutions store data on remote servers, facilitating access from any device with internet connectivity. Web-based software operates through browsers, removing installation barriers while providing flexibility. Mobile accounting apps offer on-the-go access, enabling users to manage finances directly from their smartphones or tablets.</p></p>
