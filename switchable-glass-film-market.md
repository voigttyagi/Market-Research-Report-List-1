Self-adhesive films can be easily applied to existing glass surfaces, providing flexibility and convenience for users seeking privacy control and glare reduction. In contrast, non-adhesive films require a more permanent installation, often integrated during manufacturing or renovation processes, delivering enhanced durability and performance features.
