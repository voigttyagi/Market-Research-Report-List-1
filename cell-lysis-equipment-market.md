<p><p>The cell lysis equipment market comprises various types, including homogenizers, microfluidizer processors, cell lysis kits, and other specialized tools. Homogenizers are used to break down cell structures through mechanical force, while microfluidizer processors utilize high pressure for efficient cell disruption. Cell lysis kits, often containing enzymatic solutions, provide a simpler, ready-to-use approach for lysing cells. Other equipment may include bead mills and sonicators, catering to diverse research and industrial applications requiring effective cell lysis techniques.</p></p>
