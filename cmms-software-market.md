<p><p>The CMMS software market is categorized into two primary types: Cloud-Based CMMS and On-Premises CMMS. Cloud-Based CMMS software is hosted on the vendor's servers, offering accessibility from any location with internet connectivity, facilitating remote management, lower upfront costs, and automatic updates. In contrast, On-Premises CMMS software is installed locally on a company's servers, providing greater control over data and customization but requiring higher upfront investments and ongoing maintenance. Both types cater to different organizational needs and preferences.</p></p>
