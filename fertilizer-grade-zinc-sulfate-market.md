<p><p>The fertilizer grade zinc sulfate market comprises two main types: zinc sulfate monohydrate and zinc sulfate heptahydrate. Zinc sulfate monohydrate is a concentrated form that contains one molecule of water, making it suitable for applications requiring higher purity and effectiveness in soil enrichment. On the other hand, zinc sulfate heptahydrate contains seven molecules of water, providing a more cost-effective option for larger-scale agricultural use. Both forms serve to correct zinc deficiencies in crops, enhancing growth and yield.</p></p>
