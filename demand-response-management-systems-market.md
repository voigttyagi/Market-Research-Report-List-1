<p><p>Demand Response Management Systems (DRMS) encompass two primary market types: Conventional Demand Response and Automated Demand Response. Conventional Demand Response relies on manual interventions where consumers adjust energy usage in response to utility signals, often incentivized by lower rates. In contrast, Automated Demand Response leverages advanced technologies to automatically adjust energy consumption in real-time, streamlining the process through smart grid tools and IoT connectivity. Both types aim to balance supply and demand, enhance grid reliability, and optimize energy costs.</p></p>
