<p><strong>The Sleep Tracking Technologies Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electroencephalography</li><li>Electrooculography</li><li>Electromyography</li><li>Electrocardiography</li><li>Pulse Oximetry Device</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sleep tracking technologies encompass various market types that monitor different physiological signals during sleep. Electroencephalography (EEG) measures brain waves to assess sleep stages. Electrooculography (EOG) detects eye movements, aiding in the analysis of REM sleep. Electromyography (EMG) records muscle activity, helping to identify sleep disturbances. Electrocardiography (ECG) monitors heart rate and rhythm, while pulse oximetry devices measure oxygen saturation in the blood. Together, these technologies provide comprehensive insights into sleep quality and overall health.</p></p>
