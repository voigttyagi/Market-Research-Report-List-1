<p><strong>The Audio Transistors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PNP</li><li>NPN</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The audio transistors market is primarily categorized into two types: PNP and NPN transistors. PNP transistors allow current to flow from the emitter to the collector when the base is low, making them suitable for amplifying signals in negative environments. Conversely, NPN transistors facilitate current flow from collector to emitter when the base is high, suitable for positive signal amplification. Both types play crucial roles in audio applications, affecting sound quality and amplification efficiency in various electronic devices.</p></p>
