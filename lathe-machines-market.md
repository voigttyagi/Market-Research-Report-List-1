<p><p>The lathe machine market is primarily divided into vertical and horizontal types, each serving distinct applications. Vertical lathes, also known as vertical turning centers, feature a vertical spindle and are ideal for machining large, heavy workpieces, offering enhanced stability. Horizontal lathes, on the other hand, have a horizontal spindle and are versatile for a wide range of operations, suitable for smaller parts and quicker setups. Both types are essential in various manufacturing sectors, addressing specific machining needs efficiently.</p></p>
