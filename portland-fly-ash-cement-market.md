<p><p>Portland Fly Ash Cement is classified into two main types based on the fly ash used: Class F and Class C. Cement with Class F Fly Ash typically comes from the burning of anthracite or lignite and offers enhanced durability and lower permeability, making it ideal for more challenging environmental conditions. On the other hand, Cement with Class C Fly Ash contains higher calcium and promotes quicker strength gain, making it suitable for various construction applications, including mass concrete projects.</p></p>
