<p><p>The ultrasound simulator market is categorized into three main types: VR-based, AR-based, and MR-based simulators. VR-based simulators immerse users in a completely virtual environment, enhancing training without real patients. AR-based simulators overlay digital information onto the real world, facilitating interactive learning with physical tools. MR-based simulators combine elements of both VR and AR, allowing users to interact with virtual objects while being aware of their physical surroundings. Each type aims to enhance medical training and improve diagnostic skills effectively.</p></p>
