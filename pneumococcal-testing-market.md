<p><p>The pneumococcal testing market comprises three main types: immunodiagnostics, molecular diagnostics, and point-of-care testing. Immunodiagnostics use antibody-based methods to detect pneumococcal infections, providing sensitivity and specificity. Molecular diagnostics utilize techniques like PCR to identify genetic material from the bacteria, allowing for rapid and accurate detection. Point-of-care testing offers immediate results at the site of care, facilitating timely treatment decisions. These diverse approaches enhance diagnosis and management of pneumococcal diseases, improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
