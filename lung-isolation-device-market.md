<p><p>The lung isolation device market comprises primarily two types: endobronchial blockers and lung dissociation kits. Endobronchial blockers are specialized devices used to isolate a lung during surgeries, providing a means to collapse one lung while maintaining ventilation in the other. Lung dissociation kits contain the necessary tools for the effective separation of lung function, often employed in thoracic surgeries. Both types enhance surgical visibility and reduce complications by optimizing lung management during procedures.</p></p>
