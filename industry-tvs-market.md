<p><strong>The Industry TVS Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Uni-polar TVS</li><li>Bi-polar TVS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industry TVS (Transient Voltage Suppressor) market comprises Uni-polar and Bi-polar types, each serving distinct applications. Uni-polar TVS devices are designed to protect circuits from voltage spikes in one direction, making them ideal for single-ended systems. In contrast, Bi-polar TVS devices can handle voltage transients in both positive and negative directions, suitable for AC applications or balanced circuits. The choice between these types depends on the specific needs of the electronic systems being protected, such as voltage tolerance and operational efficiency.</p></p>
