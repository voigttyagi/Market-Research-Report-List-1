<p><p>Multi-axis wafer pre-aligners are critical tools in semiconductor manufacturing, designed to precisely position wafers before further processing. There are two primary types: vacuum aligners and atmosphere aligners. Vacuum aligners utilize suction to hold wafers securely in place, ensuring high accuracy and reducing contamination risk. In contrast, atmosphere aligners rely on an inert gas environment to protect the wafer while aligning, making them suitable for sensitive materials. Both types enhance the efficiency and yield of semiconductor fabrication processes.</p></p>
