<p><ul><li>Wet Granulation Line</li><li>Dry Granulation Line</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The continuous granulation lines market is primarily categorized into wet and dry granulation lines. Wet granulation lines utilize liquid binders to agglomerate powders, enhancing payload uniformity and solubility, making them ideal for pharmaceutical applications. Conversely, dry granulation lines compact powders without moisture, improving stability and shelf life, suitable for heat-sensitive materials. Both technologies enable consistent production and scalability, catering to diverse industries seeking efficiency and quality in granule formulation processes. Their advancements drive innovations in manufacturing and product development.</p></p>
