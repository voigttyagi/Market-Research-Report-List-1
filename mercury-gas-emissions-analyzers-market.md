<p><ul><li>Cold Vapor Atomic Absorption</li><li>Cold Atomic Fluorescence</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mercury Gas Emissions Analyzers market features technologies such as Cold Vapor Atomic Absorption (CVAA) and Cold Atomic Fluorescence (CAF). CVAA measures mercury concentration by vaporizing the sample and quantifying the absorbed light at specific wavelengths, offering high sensitivity and selectivity. In contrast, CAF detects mercury by measuring the fluorescence emitted when mercury vapor is excited by a light source, allowing for rapid and accurate quantification. Both methods are essential for environmental monitoring and regulatory compliance in industries.</p></p>
