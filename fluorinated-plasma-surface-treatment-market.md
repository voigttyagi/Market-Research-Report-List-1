<p><ul><li>Low-pressure Plasma</li><li>Atmospheric Plasma</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fluorinated Plasma Surface Treatment market encompasses two main types: low-pressure plasma and atmospheric plasma. Low-pressure plasma operates in a vacuum environment, allowing for uniform treatment and deep penetration of materials, making it ideal for delicate applications like electronics and biomedical devices. Atmospheric plasma, on the other hand, functions at ambient pressure, offering versatility and ease of integration for surface modifications on larger or more varied substrates, catering to industries such as automotive and packaging.</p></p>
