<p><p>Nutrition Support Services encompass three primary market types: Oral Nutrition, Parenteral Nutrition, and Enteral Nutrition. Oral Nutrition involves dietary supplements consumed by mouth to enhance nutritional intake, often targeting individuals with inadequate diets. Parenteral Nutrition delivers nutrients directly into the bloodstream via intravenous methods, suitable for patients unable to consume food orally or through the gastrointestinal tract. Enteral Nutrition involves delivering nutrients directly to the digestive system through feeding tubes, supporting patients with functional gastrointestinal tracts but who cannot eat normally.</p></p>
