<p><p>Digital Twin Technology encompasses several market types: </p><p>1. **Parts Twin** focuses on individual components, modeling the performance and conditions of specific parts.</p><p>2. **Product Twin** represents entire products, integrating data to simulate their behavior throughout their lifecycle.</p><p>3. **Process Twin** captures operational workflows, allowing for optimization and predictive insights into manufacturing or service processes.</p><p>4. **System Twin** combines multiple interconnected components or systems, offering a holistic view and enabling comprehensive analysis and management of complex environments.</p></p>
