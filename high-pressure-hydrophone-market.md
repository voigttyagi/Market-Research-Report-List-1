<p><strong>The High-pressure Hydrophone Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Scalar High-pressure Hydrophone</li><li>Vector High-pressure Hydrophone</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-pressure hydrophone market is categorized into scalar and vector high-pressure hydrophones. Scalar high-pressure hydrophones measure sound pressure levels at specific points, providing critical data for underwater acoustic research and monitoring. In contrast, vector high-pressure hydrophones capture both pressure and particle motion, offering comprehensive information about sound waves' direction and intensity. These technologies are essential in applications such as oceanography, environmental monitoring, and maritime defense, facilitating advanced exploration and analysis of underwater sound environments.</p></p>
