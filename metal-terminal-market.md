<p><ul><li>Single Hole</li><li>Double Holes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metal terminal market consists of various types, primarily categorized into single hole and double hole terminals. Single hole terminals are designed for connecting one wire or connector, offering simplicity and efficiency in installations. In contrast, double hole terminals facilitate connections for two wires, enhancing versatility in applications requiring secure, multi-point connections. Both types are widely used in automotive, electronic, and industrial environments, ensuring reliable electrical connections while minimizing the risk of loose or incomplete contacts.</p></p>
