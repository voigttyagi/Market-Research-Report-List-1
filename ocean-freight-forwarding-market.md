<p><strong>The Ocean Freight Forwarding Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LCL</li><li>FCL</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ocean freight forwarding market is primarily divided into three types: Full Container Load (FCL), Less than Container Load (LCL), and other specialized services. FCL involves shipping an entire container dedicated to a single shipment, offering efficiency for large volumes. LCL combines shipments from multiple clients into one container, making it cost-effective for smaller loads. Other market types include roll-on/roll-off (RoRo) for vehicles and breakbulk for cargo that doesn't fit standard containers, catering to diverse shipping needs.</p></p>
