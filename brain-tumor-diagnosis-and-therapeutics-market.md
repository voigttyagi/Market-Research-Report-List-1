<p><p>The brain tumor diagnosis and therapeutics market encompasses various diagnostic methods and testing technologies. MRI and CT scans provide imaging to identify tumors, while tissue sampling involves biopsy for histopathological analysis. PET-CT scans assess metabolic activity, and cerebral arteriograms visualize blood vessels. Lumbar puncture analyzes cerebrospinal fluid for tumor markers, and molecular testing detects specific genetic mutations. EEG measures electrical activity in the brain to identify seizure activity associated with tumors, contributing to a comprehensive approach in managing brain tumors.</p></p>
