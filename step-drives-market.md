<p><p>Step drives market encompasses two primary segments: DC drives and AC drives. DC drives use direct current to control motor speeds and are ideal for applications needing precise control and high torque at low speeds. In contrast, AC drives utilize alternating current and are suited for variable speed applications. While AC drives are more versatile and efficient for larger systems, DC drives excel in specific, high-performance tasks. Both markets cater to diverse industrial needs, enhancing operational efficiency across various sectors.</p></p>
