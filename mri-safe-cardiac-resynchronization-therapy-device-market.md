<p><p>The MRI Safe Cardiac Resynchronization Therapy Device market includes devices designed to improve heart function for patients with heart failure or arrhythmias who require imaging through MRI. This market comprises two primary types: pacemakers and defibrillators. Pacemakers help manage irregular heartbeats by delivering electrical impulses to maintain a normal rhythm, while defibrillators deliver shocks to restore normal rhythm during life-threatening arrhythmias. Both types are engineered to be safe for use during MRI procedures, enhancing patient care without compromising diagnostic imaging.</p></p>
