<p><p>The two-part epoxies market consists of various types, primarily including epoxy resin/amine and epoxy resin/hardener combinations. Epoxy resin/amine systems utilize amines to initiate curing, providing enhanced toughness and chemical resistance, making them ideal for industrial applications. Conversely, epoxy resin/hardener formulations use hardeners to achieve strong adhesion and durability, commonly found in construction and automotive sectors.
