<p><p>Laboratory gas delivery systems are crucial for efficiently managing gas supply in research environments. Semi-automatic switchover systems require some manual intervention to switch between gas sources, ensuring continuous supply during operation. In contrast, fully automatic switchover systems seamlessly transition between gas sources without user intervention, enhancing reliability and minimizing downtime. Both types cater to varying operational needs, with fully automatic systems offering higher convenience and safety, while semi-automatic systems provide more cost-effective solutions for facilities with less frequent gas use.</p></p>
