<p><ul><li>Free-Blowing</li><li>Mold-Blowing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The glass blowing services market encompasses two primary techniques: free-blowing and mold-blowing. Free-blowing involves shaping molten glass through manual manipulation using tools and breath, allowing for unique, custom designs. In contrast, mold-blowing utilizes pre-formed molds to create specific shapes more efficiently, ensuring consistency in production. These techniques cater to various industries, offering creative solutions for artistic glass pieces as well as functional glassware, thereby enhancing the versatility and accessibility of blown glass products in the market.</p></p>
