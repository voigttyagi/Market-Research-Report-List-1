<p><p>The activated carbon tower market comprises two primary types: vertical and horizontal activated carbon towers. Vertical activated carbon towers are designed for space-efficient vertical installations, optimizing the removal of contaminants from air and water while allowing gravity-assisted flow. In contrast, horizontal activated carbon towers offer a broader cross-sectional area, facilitating a larger volume of treatment, making them suitable for applications requiring higher capacities. Both designs are essential in various industries for effective air and water purification.</p></p>
