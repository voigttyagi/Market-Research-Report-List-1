<p><strong>The Universal Testing Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electromechanical UTM</li><li>Hydraulic UTM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Universal Testing Machines (UTMs) are categorized primarily into two main types: electromechanical and hydraulic. Electromechanical UTMs use electric motors and mechanical components to apply loads, offering precision and versatility for testing materials like metals and plastics. They are ideal for tensile, compression, and flexural testing. Hydraulic UTMs, on the other hand, utilize hydraulic fluid to generate force, making them better suited for high-capacity testing applications, especially in large-scale or high-stress material assessments. Each type serves specific testing requirements in various industries.</p></p>
