<p><p>Blockchain as a Service (BaaS) offers two primary market models: private and public. The private model provides a secure, restricted network for organizations, allowing them to control access and maintain privacy while leveraging blockchain technology for internal processes. In contrast, the public model operates on an open network, enabling broader participation and transparency, where anyone can join the blockchain. These models cater to different needs, with private BaaS focusing on confidentiality and security, while public BaaS emphasizes accessibility and collaboration.</p></p>
