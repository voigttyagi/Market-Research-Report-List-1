<p><p>The steam accumulators market comprises two primary types: vertical and horizontal. Vertical steam accumulators are designed to optimize space efficiency and are often used in applications with limited floor area. They provide quick response times and are suitable for high-pressure steam storage. Horizontal steam accumulators, on the other hand, offer higher storage capacities and are generally easier to maintain. They are preferred in larger facilities where space is less constrained, providing stable steam supply for industrial processes.</p></p>
