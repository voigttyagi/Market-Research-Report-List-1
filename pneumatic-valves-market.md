<p><strong>The Pneumatic Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Two-Way</li><li>Three-Way</li><li>Four-Way</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pneumatic valves market is segmented into three main types: Two-Way, Three-Way, and Four-Way valves. Two-Way valves control the flow of compressed air through a single path, enabling on/off operations. Three-Way valves have one inlet and two outlets, allowing for directional control and flow switching. Four-Way valves manage air flow in two directions, commonly used in double-acting cylinders for more complex operations. Each type serves specific applications in automation and control systems, enhancing efficiency in industrial processes.</p></p>
