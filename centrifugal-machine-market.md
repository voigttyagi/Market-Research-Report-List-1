<p><p>The centrifugal machine market encompasses two main types: continuous and batch centrifugal machines. Continuous centrifugal machines operate non-stop, efficiently processing large volumes of material in a continuous flow, ideal for industrial applications needing high throughput. In contrast, batch centrifugal machines function in discrete cycles, processing a fixed amount of material per operation, offering flexibility for varied production needs. Both types serve distinct industries, including pharmaceuticals, food and beverage, and chemical processing, each meeting specific operational requirements and production goals.</p></p>
