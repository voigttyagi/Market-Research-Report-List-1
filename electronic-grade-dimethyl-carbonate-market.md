<p><p>The Electronic Grade Dimethyl Carbonate (DMC) market is primarily segmented by production methods: the Propylene Oxide Method and the Ethylene Oxide Method. The Propylene Oxide Method involves reacting propylene oxide with methanol, resulting in high-purity DMC suitable for electronics applications. Conversely, the Ethylene Oxide Method uses ethylene oxide and methanol, offering a different chemical pathway. Both methods yield high-quality DMC essential for various electronic applications, including solvents and electrolytes, catering to the growing demand in the electronics industry.</p></p>
