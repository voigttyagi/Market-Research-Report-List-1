<p><p>The network switches market is categorized into three main types: Fixed Managed, Fixed Unmanaged, and Modular Switches. Fixed Managed Switches offer configurable settings, allowing for enhanced control and monitoring of network traffic, making them ideal for complex networks. Fixed Unmanaged Switches, on the other hand, provide a straightforward plug-and-play solution without additional management features, suitable for simple networking needs.
