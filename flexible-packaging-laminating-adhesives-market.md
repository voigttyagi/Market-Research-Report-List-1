<p><p>The flexible packaging laminating adhesives market comprises three primary types: water-based, solvent-based, and solventless adhesives. Water-based adhesives are eco-friendly, utilizing water as a carrier, making them suitable for various applications involving food packaging. Solvent-based adhesives contain organic solvents, providing strong bonding but raising environmental and safety concerns. Solventless adhesives, which rely on chemical reactions for curing, offer high-performance bonding with minimal environmental impact, making them increasingly popular in sustainable packaging solutions. Each type serves specific needs in flexible packaging applications.</p></p>
