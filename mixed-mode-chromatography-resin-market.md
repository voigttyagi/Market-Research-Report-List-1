<p><p>The Mixed-Mode Chromatography Resin market encompasses various types of resins used for biomolecule separation based on multiple interactions. Ion Exchange resins utilize electrostatic attraction, allowing separation based on charge. Hydrophobic resins facilitate interaction based on hydrophobicity, separating molecules through non-polar interactions. Hydroxyapatite resins, resembling natural bone material, selectively bind proteins and nucleic acids, leveraging their affinity for phosphate groups. Each type offers distinct advantages, enhancing purification processes in biopharmaceuticals and research applications by improving resolution and yield.</p></p>
