Impedance Composite Mufflers combine multiple elements for optimal sound attenuation. Micro Perforated Plate Mufflers feature tiny holes for enhanced sound absorption. Small Hole Mufflers focus on reducing specific frequency ranges, and Active Mufflers employ electronic controls to adaptively manage noise levels in real-time.</p></p>
