<p><strong>The Building Waterproof Coating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry</li><li>Liquid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The building waterproof coating market is primarily divided into two types: dry and liquid coatings. Dry coatings often come in powder form and require mixing with a binder before application, providing a strong, durable barrier once cured. Liquid coatings, on the other hand, are ready-to-use solutions that can be easily applied using brushes, rollers, or spray equipment, offering versatility and quick-drying properties. Both types serve to protect structures from moisture damage, yet cater to different application methods and surface requirements.</p></p>
