<p><p>The tunneling machinery market primarily consists of Tunnel Boring Machines (TBMs) and roadheaders, each serving distinct functions in underground excavation. TBMs are specialized for large-scale, efficient tunneling in various soil and rock conditions, utilizing rotating blades to create smooth tunnels. In contrast, roadheaders are versatile, capable of handling softer rock and coal, equipped with a horizontally rotating cutting head for precise excavation. Both types support infrastructure projects, including transportation and utilities, enhancing efficiency and safety in tunneling operations.</p></p>
